OverTheWire

OverTheWire is a popular platform for learning and practicing security concepts through wargames, which are challenges that simulate real-world hacking scenarios. These games are designed to teach a variety of skills, ranging from basic Linux commands and scripting to more advanced techniques like binary exploitation, cryptography, and network security.
