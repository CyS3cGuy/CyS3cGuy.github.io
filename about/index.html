About

ABOUT CYS3CGUY Link to heading

CyS3cGuy is a blogging site created by Naidamair to share articles, opinions, and walkthroughs. The site’s name is derived from “Cyber Security Guy” and it was founded in 2024. Its primary focus is on IT and Cyber Security topics.

You can find walkthroughs at this site. The main differentiator between the walkthroughs in this site and the other sites is that this site tends to be more verbose than others as the author strives to provide in-depth analysis and thought process that gradually unravel the solution, rather than providing the solution directly.

WHO IS NAIDAMAIR Link to heading

Naidamair is an IT and Cyber Security enthusiast with experience in Cyber Security Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC), and Security Architecture. Although he is not a hacker, nor specifically a red teamer or blue teamer, he is deeply interested in learning new things and sharing his insights and experiences through his blog. Naidamair spends his spare time blogging, aiming to provide valuable content to others interested in the field.
